Casa Da Pereira is a small local restaurant founded in 2025, it was created by 3 culinary graduates that wanted to provide better and affordable meals after learning and understanding more about the industry. The restaurant strives to provide healthy, homely meals that cater both towards people with all dietary restrictions and preferences. They also have a selection of baked goods and desserts available throughout the day. They plan to broaden their customer base and expand the restaurant across the country. Their menu was made to accommodate people of all ages, people who are limited to vegan and vegetarianism, lactose intolerance, and allergies
